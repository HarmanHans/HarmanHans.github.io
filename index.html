About Me

My name is Harman and I really took to coding when I was taking prerequisites for my eventual major - Geography: Data Science. Within my major, the classes that really stood out to me were the ones where we worked with Python and R to visualize data, as well as the opportunity to learn machine learning. Following college, I've taken more time to develop my coding skills and apply them to my personal life. Or developing a bot that interacted with viewers in my friends Twitch chat, after noticing that they would always request links to the game he was playing but have trouble finding one oftentimes.
